About

I'm Emmanuel Effiom Duke, an interdisciplinary researcher focused on energy systems and air quality, and a full-stack developer. My research spans CFD modeling of power-plant components, machine-learning-driven energy optimization, and open digital platforms for climate and citizen science.

Alongside research, I build software — AI agents, geospatial tools, and platforms for agriculture, security, and career development, with an ongoing interest in development challenges across Nigeria and Africa.
